Who is Mora?

Discography


Birth: He was born on April 18, 1996, in Bayamón, Puerto Rico.


Musical Beginnings: He showed interest in music from a young age, learning to play the flute at six and later the piano. He briefly attended the Berklee College of Music but dropped out to focus on his music career, which he began independently in 2017 with singles like "Provocándome".

Rise to Fame: His career took off after signing with the record label Rimas Music in 2018. A key collaboration was the 2020 single "Una Vez" with Bad Bunny from the album YHLQMDLG, which gave him significant visibility. He has also collaborated with other prominent artists such as Jhayco, Feid, Sech, Yandel, and Arcángel.
Successful Producer: In addition to singing, Mora has been recognized by Billboard as one of the top 10 Latin producers and composers of urban music, participating in the production of successful songs for Bad Bunny's albums, such as YHLQMDLG and Un Verano Sin Ti.
